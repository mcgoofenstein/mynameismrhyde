Disney’s Latest ‘Cinderella’ Trailer Hints at ‘More to the Legend’

  • Article
  • Comments

  • Academy Award

  • Cate Blanchett

  • Cinderella

  • Disney

  • Downton Abbey

  • Frozen Fever

  • Game of Thrones

  • Helena Bonham Carter

  • Lily James

  • Richard Madden

  • By
  • Sarene Leeds

There is no lack of hype when it comes to Disney’s coming live-action ‘Cinderella’ movie. It has “Frozen Fever” as an opening act, it boasts a British fantasy cast of Academy Award winners and nominees (Cate Blanchett, Helena Bonham Carter), and actors from popular prestige TV dramas (Lily James from “Downton Abbey” and Richard Madden, erstwhile of “Game of Thrones“).

But with the film’s premiere date – March 13 – more than a month away, there’s no harm in Disney ramping up excitement even further. Especially when a 2015 audience has zero interest in having James’s Cinderella resemble the helpless, damsel-in-distress version from Disney’s 1950 animated original.

In a brand-new trailer released today, with the tagline “There’s more to the legend than you ever dreamed,” the film hints at a much more empowered and selfless Cinderella, one who “will protect the prince from you, no matter what becomes of me.” It’s safe to assume that the aspiring royal is speaking to her wicked stepmother (Blanchett), who, judging by this trailer – and her scheming with the evil Grand Duke (Stellan Skarsgård) – appears to be up to way more harm than just barring her stepdaughter-turned-servant from attending the ball.
